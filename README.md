## 🧬 Smart Health Profile (3-Layer Architecture)

- **Layer 1:** Demographics (age, sex, ethnicity)
- **Layer 2:** Clinical context (medications, conditions, pregnancy)
- **Layer 3:** Lifestyle factors (smoking, alcohol, exercise)

Reference ranges automatically adjust based on patient profile using:
- IADPSG/ADA gestational thresholds
- WHO/ACOG pregnancy overrides
- Age-stratified and sex-specific normal ranges

## 🛡️ Clinical Accuracy

Validated with 3-persona clinical test suite:
- ✅ Adult ♂ 44y, TSH 8.5 → HIGH (ref: 0.4–4.0)
- ✅ Adult ♀ 31y, Hb 10.5 → LOW (ref: 12.0–15.5)
- ✅ Pregnant ♀ 28y wk32, Glucose 95 → HIGH (gestational ref: 70–92)
